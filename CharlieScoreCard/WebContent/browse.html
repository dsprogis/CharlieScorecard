Find a route and add it to the list of routes that are tracked for scoring.

To find a transportation route of interest, begin by selecting the MODE of transportation (Subway, Bus, etc). The system will automatically default to the first ROUTE in the list. You may then select a ROUTE and the system will automatically default to the first TRIP in the list. A TRIP is one direction of the ROUTE, sometimes it is a variation of a ROUTE. "0" is typically "out-bound" and "1" is typically "in-bound". The system will automatically default to the first type of SERVICE on the ROUTE. A SERVICE differentiates "Weekday" from "Saturday" and "Sunday". A SERVICE also differentiates "Holidays" from "non-Holidays". Clicking the "Add Route to Collection" button will add all the TRIPS & SERVICES of a given ROUTE to the short list of ROUTES begin tracked by the system. You must have Administrator privileges to add a route for tracking.

Select a Mode of Transportation then Select a Route to reveal details.
